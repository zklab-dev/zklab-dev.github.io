Alan Li

Veteran compiler and virtual machine engineer. Previously Alan has worked for about 10 years in semiconductor companies such as IBM, MIPS, AMD, Huawei. His experience covers system software design, implementation and optimization in compilers, virtual machines and binary translators in homogeneous (CPU, DSP) and heterogeneous (GPU) computation. Alan was the maintainer of AMD’s OpenCL driver for Apple, and the author of EVM’s LLVM backend.

Alan obtained his MSc in computing science from the University of Alberta, and BE of computer science from the University of Science and Technology of China.
